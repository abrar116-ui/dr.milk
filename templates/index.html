Our Story & Heritage

Born in Nature,
Raised with Care

Dr. Milk was founded with a singular, uncompromising vision: to bring the purest, most nutrient-rich A2 milk back to your family's table.

We observed that modern dairy had lost its soul—sacrificing quality, ethics, and health for mass production. Our journey began with a commitment to ahimsa (cruelty-free) farming. Today, our open-pasture farms ensure our cows graze naturally on organic fodder.

We believe that happy, healthy cows give the sweetest, most nourishing milk. Every drop of Dr. Milk is delivered to you exactly as nature intended: untouched by human hands, free from hormones and antibiotics, and packed in eco-friendly glass bottles to preserve its natural luxury.
